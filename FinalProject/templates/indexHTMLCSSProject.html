Bupama's Brownies

Click here to go to the google doc.

Ingredients

Amount Ingredient
2 oz unsweetened chocolate, chopped
1/2 cup unsalted butter
1 1/3 cups granulated sugar
1 cup flour
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup milk
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
3 rounded tbsp unsweetened cocoa
1 1/4 cups boiling water

Baking Tips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 F.
  2. Melt chocolate in a heatproof bowl or in a double boiler.
  3. Stir until fully melted and smooth, and let cool slightly.
  4. Mix butter and half the sugar with an electric mixer until light and fluffy.
  5. Then sift flour, baking powder, and salt together.
  6. Then add sifted ingredients and milk into the butter mixture.
  7. Then use a mixer on low speed until well mixed.
  8. Use a rubber spatula and fold the chocolate and half the vanilla in.
  9. Butter and pan and evenly spread the batter inside.
  10. Stir the remaining sugars and cocoa together and sprinkle it over the batter.
  11. Add the vanilla to the water and pour slowly over the batter.
  12. Cook for about 1 hour.

Makes about 16 brownies.

Enjoy!
